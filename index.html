About Me

My name is Lev Zhitnik, I am a full-stack engineer in training with a B.Arch in Architecture & Town Planning from the Technion and an AI certificate from MIT Professional Education. My background includes pioneering computational design and data-driven urban planning, including Israel's first generative urban masterplan for Sde-Dov. I have consistently leveraged data analytics, computational design and automation to innovate and inform smarter city planning and system design.

Professional Skills

Full Stack & Programming

A full-stack developer with a solid foundation in JavaScript and React, specializing in building dynamic, responsive web applications. Proficient in React’s component-based architecture, hooks, state management, and modern ES6+ syntax. Experienced with back-end development using Node.js and Express, as well as RESTful APIs and database integration. Background in Python adds versatility across development environments. Strong understanding of client and server-side optimization ensures smooth user experiences across platforms.

Architecture & Computation

My architecture career began with a focus on computational urban planning and data-driven design. I later joined the founding team of a Smart Cities startup, where I specialized in generative design, AI, and data analysis, developing expertise in programming with specialized libraries and environments.

Graphics & Design

Since age 13, I’ve been involved in diverse graphic design projects, focusing mainly on web design. My Architecture degree honed my design skills with a strong emphasis on personal style and presentation. Along the way, I also explored video editing, 3D modeling in Blender, and basic animation, expanding my creative toolkit.
